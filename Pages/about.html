Lethi'Moto Background

Lethi'Moto was founded by three village boys in 2021 as a side hustle to provide for their families. Based in the heart of Limpopo, in a village called Nobody, Lethi'Moto aims to provide the best cleaning services for your car, using top-quality products to make your car shine for weeks. Founded by Phineus Masela (24), Lethabo Mapela (28), and Beko Dlamini (20).

Our Vision

To lead in the carwash industry, known for excellent service and consistent, high-quality results.

Our Mission

To gain more exposure and customers, ensuring all clients feel welcomed, satisfied, and leave with a clean, shiny car.
